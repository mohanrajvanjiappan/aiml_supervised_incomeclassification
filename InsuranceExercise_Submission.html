c. Is the proportion of smokers significantly different in different genders? 
    The given two variables are categorical hence the Chi-Square test is performed. 
    H0: Smoking & Gender is independent 
    H1: Smoking & Gender is not independent 
   Since P value is < 0.05 we can reject H0. The proportion of smokers significantly different in difference genders.
